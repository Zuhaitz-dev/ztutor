Make absolutely sure you indent your recipe lines with a Hard TAB, not spaces! Make will throw a "missing separator" error if you use spaces.
---
For the linking rule (making the executable from `.o` files), use:
	$(REDIS_LD) -o $@ $^
---
For the compilation rule (making `.o` files from `.c` files), use:
	$(REDIS_CC) -MMD -o $@ -c $<
---
The `.PHONY` directive should look exactly like this: `.PHONY: clean`
